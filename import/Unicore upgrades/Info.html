This is a catalog of Unicore upgrade BIOSes from Evergreen Spectra CD. There are 3337 images in the catalog, around half of them are identified. With the exception of a few images, all of this is meant for Socket 5 and Socket 7 motherboards.

The images can be found here: https://www.dropbox.com/sh/f5neak4900zbijt/AACTyWzz9FbiJENPBNHixCZka?dl=0

Also check out the MR BIOS catalog here, it covers a much wider range of hardware: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1vtPsoKNyGD3ujMWK_G7ldm7G-jBiyPGXZOp8ZeyuFf8/edit#gid=1763344312
